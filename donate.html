How I Price

Pricing is based on actual sold comparables, not listed prices.

vs

I believe your clothes deserve a real chance at a new life — and that starts with honest pricing. Every item is priced based on actual recent sales of similar pieces, because what something sells for and what someone hopes to get for it are often two very different things. My goal is always a fair price that moves your item into loving hands quickly.
